## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use PaceBeat:

### 2.1 Personal Information

Collected when you create an account or contact us:

- Name  
- Email address  

### 2.2 Location Data

Collected when you grant location permission:

- **GPS location during runs** (e.g., route, start/end point, distance, speed)  

This data is used to provide core functions of the app (run tracking, pace/cadence analytics, maps).

### 2.3 Health & Fitness Data

Collected through your device sensors or OS APIs (where permissions are granted):

- Steps  
- Pace  
- Cadence  
- Other run-related metrics (time, distance, speed, etc.)

We do **not** collect medical records or clinical health data. All fitness data is used only to provide and improve app functionality and analytics.

### 2.4 Device Information

Automatically collected for analytics and advertising:

- Device model, operating system, and app version  
- IP address and general region (not exact address)  
- Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)  
- Crash logs and performance data  

### 2.5 Purchase History

Collected when you make in-app purchases or subscriptions:

- Purchase type (e.g., subscription tier, upgrade)  
- Transaction status (successful/failed)  
- Non-sensitive purchase metadata (via **RevenueCat** and app stores)  

We **do not** process or store full payment card numbers; payments are handled by Google Play, Apple App Store, and RevenueCat.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **To provide and operate the App**
   - Track runs, cadence, pace, and related performance metrics  
   - Display maps and GPS-based statistics  
   - Maintain your account, preferences, and training history  

2. **To improve and personalize the App**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve features and performance  
   - Customize in-app experience based on your activity  

3. **To show ads (for free users)**
   - Use device and ad-related information to show relevant ads via **Google AdMob**  

4. **To manage subscriptions and purchases**
   - Verify purchases, manage subscription status, and restore purchases using **RevenueCat**  

5. **To ensure safety, security, and legal compliance**
   - Prevent fraud, misuse, or violations of our terms  
   - Comply with laws, regulations, or legal requests  

6. **To communicate with you**
   - Send service-related notifications (e.g., changes to terms, critical app updates)  
   - Respond to support or feedback inquiries

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to provide core functionality. These services may collect and process data as described below. Their use of information is governed by their own privacy policies.

### 5.1 Google Firebase

Used for:

- **Authentication** (account login/sign-up)  
- **Database / Cloud Storage** (storing user profile data, run history, metrics)  

Firebase may process:

- Email, user ID, device info, in-app events  

### 5.2 Google AdMob (Advertising)

Used to display ads for free users.

AdMob may collect:

- Advertising ID  
- Device information, approximate location, app usage data  

This data can be used to provide **personalized** or **non-personalized** ads, depending on your choices and regional requirements.

You can reset or limit ad tracking in your device settings.

### 5.3 RevenueCat (In-App Purchases & Subscriptions)

Used to:

- Validate and manage in-app purchases and subscriptions  
- Sync entitlements across devices  

RevenueCat receives purchase-related data from app stores (order IDs, product IDs, subscription status) but does **not** receive your full payment card information.

### 5.4 Google Maps (Location Services)

Used to:

- Display your running route and map visuals within the app  

Google Maps may receive your location data or map requests to provide map tiles, routes, and related services.
